About Voico Chrome Extension

If you're constantly typing out lengthy emails or find yourself struggling to keep up with note-taking during meetings, Voico is the tool for you. All you need to do is install it from the Chrome Web Store, and you're all set. Let's walk through how to get started with Voico.

First things first, after installation, head over to the extension options page(watch the video if you don't know where this is). Here, you'll be setting up your preferred language and your OpenAI API key. There's a broad array of languages to choose from, catering to users from all around the world. Once you've picked your language, it's time to enter your API key. Hit the save button, and you'll receive a notification confirming that your credentials have been saved. Remember, this setup is a one-time process.

Why is Voico great?

  1. User-Friendly: Voico is straightforward and easy to use. Simply install the extension, click on the record button, and speak into your microphone. Voico will accurately transcribe your voice into text.
  2. Universal: Voico works on any website. Just click on the input box where you want your transcribed text to appear before you start recording. We ensure that your thoughts are accurately transcribed and placed exactly where you want them.
  3. Always Accessible: In case a website doesn't permit direct input from Voico, don't worry. We provide you with a copy of the last five transcripts. All you need to do is click the 'expand more' button on Voico to access them.
  4. Customizable: Voico offers a settings page where you can select your preferred language. This customization enhances transcription accuracy and speed, ensuring Voico caters to your unique needs.
  5. Free: Voico is absolutely free. All we require from you is an API key from OpenAI to access their speech-to-text service. Rest assured, your API key is never stored and is only used during the transcription process. Any charges incurred are from OpenAI, who offer new customers $5 of free credits, equivalent to about 7-8 hours of free transcription.

Voico is designed to make your online interactions efficient and seamless. Install Voico today and experience the magic of hands-free typing!
